    Prof. Dr. R. Logeswaran N. Rajasvaran

    • Faculty of Computing, Engineering & Technology

    Professor Dr. R. Logeswaran is an academic with 21 years of experience in a number of institutions both locally and abroad. He studied his B.Eng (Hons) Computing at Imperial College London, United Kingdom, and completed his Master of Engineering Science (M.Eng.Sc.) as well as Ph.D. (Engineering) at Multimedia University, Malaysia. He is a Novell Certified Linux Professional, and also a certified IC Digital Citizen and trainer. His areas of interests are mainly in multimedia data processing, data compression, neural networks, natural user interfaces and big data / data science, with over 130 publications in books, peer-reviewed journals and international conference proceedings. He enjoys supervising student projects at various levels from undergraduate to Masters and PhD, ensuring that proper research methodology and good teaching & learning practices are gained.

    Prof. Loges is a full Professor and Head of the Research Committee at the Faculty of Computing, Engineering and Technology, as well as a core member of the Asia Pacific Centre for Analytics (APCA) at the Asia Pacific University of Technology and Innovation (APU), where he also served as the Dean of Graduate Studies. Prior to that, he was the Dean of Engineering, Science and Technology at Nilai University and before that, the Acting Dean of Engineering and Deputy Dean of Postgraduate Studies at Multimedia University, where he has been an Adjunct Professor since 2011.

    A recipient of several scholarships, including Telekom Malaysia, he was recognized as the JCS 75th Anniversary Scholar for his Masters achievements and later went on to do his post-doctoral research in Seoul, Korea under the Brain Gain Malaysia international fellowship & post-doctoral programme as well as a Brain Korea21 post-doctoral scholar. A Senior Member of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE), he is actively involved as the Vice Chair of the IEEE Signal Processing Society Malaysia Chapter and is a reviewer of numerous journals and conferences. His efforts in the voluntary services over the past 20 years has also gained him the Pingat Kebaktian (PK) medal for long and commendable service from the Malaysian government.

    Prof. Loges has been a Keynote Speaker, Guest of Honour and Invited Speaker to a number of international conferences over the years, where he speaks on Data Science. He has also conducted trainings and short courses on Data Science locally and abroad. Additionally, he was involved is the establishment of the first Masters level postgraduate programme in Data Science and Business Analytics in Malaysia. He teaches on and supervises programmes and research projects in Data Science at the Masters and PhD levels.

    Prof. Dr. Mandava Rajeswari

    • Faculty of Computing, Engineering & Technology

    Professor in the Data Science group at Faculty of Computing Engineering & Technology, Asia Pacific University. Mandava is a retired Professor of School of Computer Sciences, University Sains Malaysia. She has 35 years of professional career with both breadth and depth in several areas of expertise and has made significant contributions in these areas. With ever new exciting areas emerging, for example Data Science, Deep learning, IoT etc., the key for success and sustainability, she is excited to be part of the Data Science group at Asia Pacific University who has pioneered in establishing Data Science academic programs.
    Currently Mandava’s passion is in Data science with an intention to research and extend machine learning and deep learning techniques to multimodal domains of image, and numeric data analytics. Her teaching courses include Machine Learning, Optimization in Data Science

    Mandava’s research has spanned from machine vision, robotics, image analysis, medical imaging, artificial intelligence, computational intelligence, data mining through several research projects. While my major domain has been image related, her focus is on the core methods that are applicable to many other domains. These core methods are: Pattern Recognition, Artificial Intelligence, Datamining, Optimization and Meta-heuristics. Some of the specific areas are clustering, classification, fuzzy and rough sets, nature inspired metaheuristics, random walks, variational methods, graph cuts etc.

    Throughout her career Mandava has pioneered several areas of research in Malaysia. These include machine vision in in late 80s, robot vision in early 90s, medical image research, computer aided diagnosis solutions and medical image related IT products through 2010. In 2015, started with Neuroimaging with specific focus on Diffusion based imaging with a primary objective to extract, visualise and examine brain white matter fiber tracts to assist in the early diagnosis of neurodegenerative diseases and assist neuro surgery.

    Dr. Ho Ming Kang

    • Faculty of Computing, Engineering & Technology

    Dr. Ho completed his PhD in Mathematics in 2014 and currently a lecturer in Asia Pacific University of Technology and Innovation (APU). His research activities mainly on data analysis generally, such as regression modeling, time series and forecasting, survival analysis, missing data analysis, and with a growing interest in machine learning such as statistical classification.

    He conducted a few workshops in APU, mainly on analysing data using SPSS and R. Besides, he also conducted workshops on developing analytical skills in relation to Operational Research practices in organisational decision making. Topics including linear programming, interger programming, network models, transportation modeling, and goal programming.

    Dr. Preethi Subramanian

    • Faculty of Computing, Engineering & Technology

    Dr. Preethi is currently working as a Lecturer and an Academic Leader for the Computer Science and Software Engineering cluster which is under the School of Computing at Asia Pacific University of Technology & Innovation in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. She is also experienced in administering databases and campus management systems for the education industry where she skillfully worked with her team for a few years in the past.

    The initial years of her tertiary education began with a B. Tech in Information Technology from Sri Krishna College of Engineering and Technology, a reputable institution under Anna University in India. She furthered her studies with a Master in Business Administration (MBA) with a specialization in Human Resource and Marketing from Thiagarajar School of Management. Down the lane, in the year 2017, she happily received her Doctorate of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in the field of Informatics from Malaysia University of Science and Technology. Her research was in enhancing telecommunication customer experience using data integration, analytics and visualization approach. She began her formal career as a Research Assistant and furthered on to being a lecturer and head of program in other private universities in Malaysia. Matured her teaching skills and exposure by teaching Degree programmes under foreign and local universities in Malaysia. She is a continuous learner who is actively pursuing new technologies in IT to adapt to the changing needs of the industry. She enjoys presenting her research in International conferences and has won the best paper award twice in 2015 and 2016 for her exemplary work. Furthermore, she is a subject matter expert in the field of databases, data science and analytics. She was honored by Pearson UK Publishers and received an invitation to be the content reviewer for their text book on Database Systems by authors named Connolly and Begg. She has reviewed the content of this popular text book which is a standard in this field across several countries.

    She has around 9 years of experience in teaching in the higher education sector in Malaysia. Her teaching expertise includes modules related to Database systems, Data management, Data mining, Analytics, E-Commerce, Business Intelligence and Data Visualization. She has also been certified in Big Data Fundamentals and Tableau for Data Science at the Advanced Level.
